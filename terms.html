Refunds and Cancellations

  2. Refund Process (If Approved by Seller): Sellers can issue refunds directly from their Razorpay Dashboard or via API. Razorpay supports instant refunds and refund tracking.

  4. If Seller is Unresponsive: Allow 7–10 business days (physical goods) or 1 day (digital goods). If unresolved, contact your bank to raise a dispute. Razorpay will coordinate with banks.

Note: Razorpay is a payment processor; refund and cancellation requests must be initiated with the seller.
