Welcome to S M Traders

We are a forward-thinking trading company based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, specializing in the efficient and transparent trade of rice and pulses — two of the most essential staples in global nutrition.

We bridge the gap between farmers, producers, and international markets, offering a seamless supply chain for commodities such as basmati and non-basmati rice (raw, boiled, steam, and broken), as well as a wide variety of lentils, chickpeas, peas, and beans. Our goal is simple: to deliver quality products, backed by fair pricing, timely delivery, and reliable partnerships.

With a dedicated team and deep market insights, we help buyers and suppliers navigate global trade with ease. By leveraging modern trading platforms and digital tools, we ensure price transparency, risk management through futures contracts, and sustainable sourcing practices that contribute to long-term food security.

  • Pulses: Lentils, Chickpeas, Peas, Beans
